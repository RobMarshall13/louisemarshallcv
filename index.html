About Me

What do I do?

As an experienced and skilled personal trainer I enjoy helping people learn to believe in there own abilities by giving them the tools to be a happy and healthier version of themselves. Seeing my clients achieve their goals gives me great satisfaction and continues to motivate me to be an even better trainer.

How do I do it?

Following a technique first approach to my personal training sessions ensures my clients get an effective, safe and injury free fitness experience. I have a very enthusiastic and professional approach. As an ex-professional dancer I always ensure to utilise my performance experience in all my contact with members and clients for both my PT and classes.

Why Hire Me?

As an excellent comunicator I thrive working in bright and enthuseastic teams and I am also confident when working solo. I have an excellent track record in member recruitment, retention and have always maintained a high conversion rate when conducting initial PT consultations.
